Top 10 Marvel Movies

  1. Avengers: Endgame (2019)

    After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers must assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

  2. Iron Man (2008)

    Tony Stark, a genius billionaire playboy, is kidnapped and forced to build a weapon for his captors. Instead, he builds an armored suit and escapes to fight for justice.

  3. The Avengers (2012)

    When an unexpected enemy threatens the world, Nick Fury, director of the international peacekeeping agency S.H.I.E.L.D., brings together a team of super-powered individuals to fight back.

  4. Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

    When an unexpected enemy threatens the world, Nick Fury, director of the international peacekeeping agency S.H.I.E.L.D., brings together a team of super-powered individuals to fight back.

  5. Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

    A group of misfits must unite to protect the galaxy from an evil force.
